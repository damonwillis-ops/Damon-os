# Values & Decision Filters

These are context for advisor reasoning — not hard gates. Advisor surfaces options that conflict with these
and explains the case for them. Final call is always mine.

## Non-Negotiables
- Location: Remote or NWA-local only. No relocation.
- Honesty: Never fabricate credentials, overreach claims, or misrepresent experience.
- Conservative search: Employed at Walmart. Nothing sends automatically. All outreach requires my approval.
- Family first: Career decisions that require significant sacrifice of family time need a compelling case.

## Strong Preferences
- Comp: At or above current. Exceptions require strong non-comp upside (equity, title trajectory, mission).
- Role fit: Converged physical + InfoSec or pure physical security leadership. Pure IT = weak lane.
- Company size: Enterprise-scale preferred — that's where my CPP + Walmart experience lands with impact.

## Decision Heuristics
- Would the reader feel they decided I'm the fit, or that they were sold? Must feel like their decision.
- Every impact claim must be anchored to enterprise scale. No vague assertions.
- Lead with PSIM full-lifecycle ownership for physical security / GSOC roles.
