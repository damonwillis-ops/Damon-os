# Decision: Consulting-First Revenue Strategy

**Decided:** 2026-06-22

## The Decision
Lead with physical security advisory consulting as primary near-term revenue play. Use consulting to fund runway, validate SAIP features, and convert clients to SaaS pilots. Groundtruth builds the public audience. SAIP is the eventual product. Consulting is the bridge.

**Why:** Fastest path to first dollar (60-90 days vs 12-18 months for Groundtruth/SAIP cold). Zero additional risk — Walmart salary continues. No cold outreach needed — warm network from 14 years enterprise physical security.

## How to Apply
- Every session that touches career or income questions: anchor to this strategy
- Consulting comes before SAIP feature build debates
- Groundtruth content should support consulting positioning (practitioner voice, not publisher)
- Any Director job search conversations: ask whether the role beats consulting income potential
